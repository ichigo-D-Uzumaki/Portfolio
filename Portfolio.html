About me

Design with purpose,

I'm Alex — a product designer based in San Francisco with 8 years of experience working at the intersection of design, technology, and human behaviour.

I believe the best products are those that feel invisible — so intuitive they never get in the way of what people are trying to do. I bring this philosophy to everything from early-stage startups to enterprise redesigns.
